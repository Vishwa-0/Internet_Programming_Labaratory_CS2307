Ticket Reservation System

Welcome! This is your all-in-one stop to book various tickets.

Our system lets you reserve tickets for Movies, Events, Buses, and Trains. Each section is tailored to collect only the details you need for that specific booking. Just fill in the form, click "Book", and you'll get a confirmation alert!

• Movie Reservation

Book tickets for the latest movies. Check which movies are showing, at which theatre, and on what date.
You'll need: Name, Age, Date, Movie name, Theatre, and Number of seats.

• Event Reservation

Book tickets for concerts, musicals, and other events. Find events by name, venue, and date.
You'll need: Name, Age, Date, Event name, Venue, and Number of seats.

• Bus Reservation

Reserve bus tickets for your travel. Choose your source, destination, and travel date.
You'll need: Name, Age, Date, Source, Destination, and Number of seats.

• Train Reservation

Book train tickets easily. Select your source station, destination, and travel date.
You'll need: Name, Age, Date, Source, Destination, and Number of seats.
